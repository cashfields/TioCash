*wildcard numbers are: *1 , *3 , *7 , *9.
Theleast siginaficant digit of anysize number makes the wildcard value.

Pronounce it like this:
"star wildcard 1" , "star wildcard 3" , "star wildcard 7" , "star wildcard 9"

The*fam (*family) numbers are subsets of *wildcard numbers.
*fam01 , *fam07 , *fam11 , *fam13 , *fam17 , *fam19 , *fam23 , and *fam29

Pronounce it like this:
"star fam 1" , "star fam 7" , "star fam 11" , . . . , "star fam 29"


*fam pairs - by 10's and 30's
1) Brothers - seperated by 10
*fam01 and *fam11 - parent *1 (|01 - 11| = 10)
*fam07 and *fam17 - parent *7 (|07 - 17| = 10)
*fam13 and *fam23 - parent *3 (|13 - 23| = 10)
*fam19 and *fam29 - parent *9 (|19 - 29| = 10)


2) Cousins (compliments that add to 30)
*fam01 and *fam29 - (parent *1 and parent *9) (01 + 29 = 30)
*fam07 and *fam23 - (parent *7 and parent *3) (07 + 23 = 30)
*fam11 and *fam19 - (parent *1 and parent *9) (11 + 19 = 30)
*fam13 and *fam17 - (parent *3 and parent *7) (13 + 17 = 30)

Each one of these pairs (brothers and cousins) will have special meaning later.

As we start out the pairs that add up to 30 (compliments) will be our first focus.

In the end , we will take a closer look at the 30's to look for a signature.

The final folder "99_just_another_30" - is the solution to finding prime numbers.

The next prime number is as close as 'just another 30' from the last prime. Living within the methodology of *wildcard and *fam the concept of 30 and the first four primes ( 1 , 2 , 3 , 5 ) and the multiplication of the first four primes ( 1 * 2 * 3 * 5 ) is a set pattern to determining if a number is prime. This unknown number is PS (Prime Suspect). Applying the sorts , and seperation , and using color to help delinate different families of numbers.

There are parents ; the *wildcard numbers ( *1 , *3 , *7 , *9 ) - any number that ends with these digits.

There are brothers ; any number seperated by ten (10).
So , *fam01 and *fam11 are brothers
as are *fam07 and *fam17
as are *fam13 and *fam23
and finally *fam19 and *fam29

Then the cousins , these compilment each other by adding up to thirty (30) br> So , *fam01 and *fam29 are cousins
as are *fam07 and *fam23
as are *fam11 and *fam19
and finally *fam13 and *fam17
